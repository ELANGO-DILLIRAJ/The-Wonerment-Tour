Eligibility & Rules

Please read the following guidelines carefully before booking your tickets. Your safety and comfort are our priority.

👤

Age Requirements

To ensure the safety and enjoyment of all attendees, the following age restrictions apply:

  • ! Attendees must be 16 years or older for General Admission (standing zones).
  • ! Children aged 6–15 may attend seated sections only and must be accompanied by an adult (18+) at all times.
  • ✕ No children under 5 years old are permitted due to high sound levels (100dB+) and pyrotechnic effects.
  • i Valid government-issued photo ID with date of birth is required at the entrance for age verification.
  • i Infants and toddlers will be denied entry without exception, regardless of ticket purchase.
🏥

Medical & Health Advisory

The Wonderment Tour features high-energy production elements. Please consider the following health advisories:

  • ⚡ Strobe lighting and lasers will be used extensively. Attendees with epilepsy or photosensitive conditions should consult a physician before attending.
  • ♥ Guests with pacemakers or heart conditions are advised to avoid front-row and speaker-adjacent zones due to extreme bass frequencies.
  • ! Pregnant attendees are recommended to use seated sections and avoid standing-only zones.
  • ! Sound levels may exceed 100 dB. Complimentary earplugs are available at all venue information desks.
  • + First aid stations will be available inside all venues. On-site paramedics will be on standby throughout the event.
  • i Attendees requiring wheelchair accessibility or special seating arrangements should contact the venue directly at least 48 hours before the event.
🚫

Prohibited Items

The following items are strictly prohibited inside all concert venues. Security checks will be enforced at every entry point.

⚒️ Weapons of any kind
🔪 Sharp objects & knives
📷 Professional cameras
🎥 Recording equipment
🧨 Fireworks & flares
🍺 Outside food & drinks
🎒 Backpacks (>A4 size)
🤳 Selfie sticks
💡 Laser pointers
🏳️ Large banners & flags
🪑 Folding chairs
🐕 Pets (except service)
⚖️

Venue & Country Regulations

Each venue operates under its country's local laws. Please familiarize yourself with the regulations for your chosen event location:

🇮🇳 India — Chennai & Mumbai

  • Alcohol consumption is permitted only within designated licensed zones inside the venue.
  • Smoking and vaping are strictly prohibited inside the stadium premises as per Indian law.
  • Events must adhere to noise regulations; the show will conclude by 22:30 IST.
  • Aadhaar Card or Passport is accepted as valid photo ID for entry.
  • Public intoxication may result in immediate removal from the venue.

🇦🇪 United Arab Emirates — Dubai

  • Alcohol is available only in designated licensed areas within Coca-Cola Arena.
  • Modest dress code is recommended; offensive clothing or slogans are not permitted.
  • Photography for personal use is allowed; commercial recording is prohibited.
  • Attendees must carry their Emirates ID or Passport for security verification.
  • Public displays of affection should be kept to a minimum in accordance with local customs.
  • Narcotics and controlled substances carry severe legal penalties under UAE law.

🇬🇧 United Kingdom — London

  • The O2 Arena is a smoke-free venue. Smoking is only permitted in designated outdoor areas.
  • Challenge 25 policy: anyone appearing under 25 will be asked for ID when purchasing alcohol.
  • Anti-social behavior, including standing on seats, may result in ejection without refund.
  • Bag searches are conducted at entry. Only small bags (A4 size or smaller) are allowed.
  • UK driving licence or Passport is accepted for age verification.

🇺🇸 United States — New York

  • Legal drinking age is 21. Valid state-issued ID or Passport is required for alcohol purchases.
  • Madison Square Garden is a smoke-free facility. No smoking, vaping, or e-cigarettes inside.
  • All attendees are subject to security screening, including metal detectors and bag inspections.
  • Re-entry is not permitted. Once you leave the venue, your ticket is void.
  • ADA-compliant accessible seating is available; contact the box office for accommodations.
